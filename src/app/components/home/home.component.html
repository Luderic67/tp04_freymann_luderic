HOME

Here you can see a resume of all new things for this TP04

CART

Browse the catalog among a huge list of products, filter and sort the products to find the one you need!

Add them to your cart to see the total price, or to be able to buy them later.

Your basket is saved automatically, refresh the page to see it!

ADDRESSES

Enter your addresses to receive your deliveries as soon as possible!

Manage your addresses, delete them and add new ones, you can create your own address list.

Just like the cart, the addresses are saved automatically, refresh the page to see it.

DETAILS

This new feature allows you to view product details, part numbers, images, descriptions, and more.

The detail page of a product is accessible from the catalog, or from the basket if the product is already there.

It is also possible to add a product to the basket directly from its details page.
